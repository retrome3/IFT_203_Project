About LearnSmart Tutoring

Our mission, vision, and teaching philosophy

Our Story

Founded in 2023, LearnSmart Tutoring was born from a simple observation: every student learns differently, yet most educational systems treat them the same. We saw students struggling not because they lacked ability, but because they lacked the right approach.

Our founder, Bello Mubarak, spent years in traditional education witnessing bright students fall through the cracks. Determined to make a difference, Bello created LearnSmartâ€”a tutoring service that adapts to the student, not the other way around.

Our Mission

To empower students to become confident, independent learners by providing tailored educational support that goes beyond just homework help. We focus on developing critical thinking skills, building academic confidence, and fostering a lifelong love of learning.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every student has access to personalized education that recognizes their unique strengths and learning style. Where struggling in one subject doesn't define a student's entire academic journey.

Teaching Philosophy

  • Student-Centered Learning: Every lesson is customized to the individual student
  • Growth Mindset: We believe intelligence can be developed through dedication
  • Mastery Over Memorization: Focus on deep understanding rather than rote learning
  • Positive Reinforcement: Building confidence through celebration of small victories
  • Real-World Application: Connecting academic concepts to practical situations
